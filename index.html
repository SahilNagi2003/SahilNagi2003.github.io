UNSC Purpose

The UNSC (United Nations Space Command) serves as humanity's military, scientific, and exploratory body in the Halo universe. Their goals include:

  • Defending Earth and human colonies from external threats.
  • Exploring space and advancing humanityâ€™s technological reach.
  • Fighting against the Covenant and other hostile factions.
  • Upholding peace and order within human territories.
  • Reclaiming and securing planets overrun by the Flood.

Covenant Purpose

The Covenant is an alliance of alien species united by religious beliefs and military conquest. Their goals include:

  • Seeking and activating the Halo rings to achieve the "Great Journey."
  • Subjugating or eradicating any species that oppose them, including humanity.
  • Following the Prophets' teachings based on Forerunner technology and relics.
  • Destroying the Flood to prevent its spread across the galaxy.
  • Maintaining the alliance between various Covenant species under theocratic rule.

Flood Purpose

The Flood is a parasitic species that threatens all life in the galaxy. Their goals include:

  • Infecting and assimilating all life forms, particularly sentient species.
  • Overwhelming military forces, including the UNSC and Covenant.
  • Spreading across the galaxy to consume all biomass.
  • Escaping containment and reaching new worlds for further infection.
  • Becoming a Gravemind, the Flood's collective intelligence and leader.
